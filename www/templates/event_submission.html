Submit an Event to the GoLocal! Calendar

It's completely free to submit an event to the calendar and submissions are loaded in realtime. There are a few rules, though:

  • You have to be a registered GoLocal! -Fayette user. Please Login or Sign Up before attempting to submit an event.
  • You are limited one (1) event per month and four (4) per calendar year.
  • Your submission MUST not include any inappropriate, profane, abusive, or otherwise unacceptable language or links to inappropriate material.

    • Violators of these rules will have their submissions removed and may have their accounts blocked.
    GoLocal! reserves the right to edit or remove any submission at its sole discretion.
